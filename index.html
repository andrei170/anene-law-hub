What I Found

The whole site is telling Google to stay away

This is not a content problem or a small SEO tweak. Every page of anene-law.com is carrying a single line of code that removes it from search results.

Critical

Every page is set to "noindex, nofollow"

I checked the homepage, the Fathers' Rights page, and a team bio page. All three serve this instruction to Google in their code:

<meta name="robots" content="noindex, nofollow">

In plain English: that tag tells Google "do not list this page, and do not follow its links." It is almost certainly the WordPress "Discourage search engines" box left switched on. The fix takes minutes, but until it is flipped, nothing else you do in search can work.

The proofA "site:anene-law.com" search returns only the hidden RSS feed. Your real pages are not in Google's index.
